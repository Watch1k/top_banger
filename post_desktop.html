This Carolina Panther Is Airbnb-ing His Place For Super Bowl Sunday

Roman Harper, a safety for the Carolina Panthers, put his Charlotte apartment on Airbnb for someone to rent while he's busy "playing the Denver Broncos at Super Bowl 50." Yes, that's what it actually says.

A rep for Airbnb confirmed that this in fact real, and the one-bedroom, two-bath apartment and all its amenities are actually available for a night.

A night in the pad will cost you $5,000, and will be going to Harper's Hope 41 Foundation. Oh, and you get a signed football.

The signed baseball is just a little extra. The actual apartment is where it's at. Rental of the place includes a 24-hour concierge service, tennis and basketball court, and a junior Olympic pool.

It also has a HDTV screening room with surround-sound – just in case you forgot it was Super Bowl Sunday.
